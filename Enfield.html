                Local authority analysis: Enfield

                Analysing the impact of the pandemic on children’s speech development

                Children entering school in the year after the first lockdown are showing a big increase in speech, language and communication (SLC) needs, according to analysis of DfE data. Find out how the numbers changed in Enfield.

                In Enfield the number of children needing support for speech, language and communication in Year 1 dropped from 278 in 2015/16 to 216 in 2021/22.

                The number of children needing support for speech, language and communication in Year 1 in 2021/22 dropped by -22.86% compared to the previous academic year. This is below the the national rise of 9.81%. The authority ranks 32 in the region out of 33 authorities.
