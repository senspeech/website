Local authority analysis: Telford and Wrekin

Analysing the impact of the pandemic on children’s speech development

Children entering school in the year after the first lockdown are showing a big increase in speech, language and communication (SLC) needs, according to analysis of DfE data. Find out how the numbers changed in Telford and Wrekin.

In Telford and Wrekin the number of children needing support for speech, language and communication in Year 1 rose from 139 in 2015/16 to 192 in 2021/22.

The number of children needing support for speech, language and communication in Year 1 in 2021/22 rose by 46.56% compared to the previous academic year. This is higher than the national rise of 9.81%. The authority ranks 1 in the region out of 14 authorities.
